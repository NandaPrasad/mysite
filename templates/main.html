Our Goals

Our team's goal is to reduce the amount of time it takes to get places by car. The amount of traffic in and around Falcon Pointe is bad, and it is preventing people from doing their jobs and succeeding.

What are we doing?

We are trying to reduce the amount of traffic by informing people where the most amount of traffic is. By identifying the troubling areas, we can help people to avoid it, hereby reducing the amount of traffic, and dispersing it out.

How Will We Accomplish it?

We decided to make this website in order to inform you about the traffic. We asked people about where the most amount of traffic is, and we compare it to the business hours of the surrounding buildings. After comparing it, we document when and where the traffic is centered, and put it into this site.
